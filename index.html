Introduction


My research proposal examines the different methods used to combat human trafficking. Many individuals have different ideas on whether criminalizing, decriminalizing, and legalizing prostitution decreases the amount of sex trafficking cases. I want to find information on human trafficking by reviewing previous literature on the effects of legalization, decriminalization, and criminalization. By analyzing the different legality of prostitution, I can determine which legislation is more effective in reducing human trafficking and helping survivors. This research is particularly important to me because I am currently studying criminology. Currently, I’m wanting to advocate for survivor’s rights, but I must have comprehensive knowledge on the best preventative methods. Additionally, want to become a criminal prosecutor for human trafficking and child exploitation cases in the future. Having knowledge of previous literature on this and formulating an analysis will be vital to my future career choices and in providing help to human trafficking survivors.
